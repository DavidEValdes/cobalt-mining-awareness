Cobalt Mining Awareness

About Cobalt Mining

Cobalt mining, while pivotal in driving the technological revolution and green energy transition, has raised substantial environmental and ethical concerns, particularly in regions like Africa. The extraction processes can lead to water pollution, depletion, and contribute to harmful ecological impacts, thereby demanding a balanced, sustainable approach to cobalt production.

Environmental and Social Impact

Cobalt mining in Africa entails not only environmental but also social repercussions, disrupting local communities and ecosystems.
